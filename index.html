Bahaa Aldeeb

I am currently working as a research staff member in the Robot Perception and Manipulation Laboratory lead by Professor Karthik Desingh at the University of Minnesota Twin-city.

Prior to that I had earned my Masters in Robotics in 2020 and a Bachelors in Computer Engineering in 2017, from University of Michigan. Through the years I also worked on autonomous vehicle perception and assisted in running laboratory courses on autonomous robotics alongside my studies.

I am interested in robot perception and ways to allow the embodied agents to utilize such knowledge to operate in the real world. Self-supervised methods for train towards perception for manipulation piques my interest. I join professor Desingh's lab in an interest to research and explore that realm and am hope to pursue this topic as part of a Ph.D. next year.
